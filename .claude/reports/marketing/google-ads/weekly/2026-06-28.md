### The Big Picture

This is the best week in over a month: three conversions, spend down to $264, and two won deals worth nearly $31,000 in revenue, both attributed to paid search. The CPA of $88 is the closest to the $80 target in the past five weeks, and mobile produced zero conversions again but the 8a-12p block generated two conversions, the strongest single time block in the dataset. The improvement is real but fragile. Impression share fell from 27 to 14 percent, the lowest in the five-week window, meaning the account is winning fewer auctions than ever even as it converts better on the ones it does win. All four campaigns still lose 82 to 90 percent of available auctions to ad rank, the same structural problem that has persisted every week. The two QS-3 keywords flagged in every prior report remain unresolved, and "sprinkler valve repair" now joins "lee engineering company" as new waste to cut.

### Anomalies & Watch Items

- Impression share dropped to 14 percent this week, the lowest in the dataset, while lost-to-budget stayed at zero across all campaigns. The account is winning fewer auctions than ever, but because spend is frozen and bids cannot be raised, the only available remedy is quality score improvement.
- Mobile has produced zero conversions for five consecutive weeks while taking 71 percent of spend this week. Desktop converted at 21.4 percent. This gap is now so large and consistent that a broken or severely degraded mobile form or landing page remains the most probable explanation. This has been flagged every week and has not been acted on.
- "sprinkler valve repair" spent $5.69 with no conversion and is flagged as waste. Sprinkler valve repair is a legitimate irrigation service Black Hill offers, so this is not a category exclusion. However two clicks and zero conversions is thin data. Worth adding as a negative only if the trend continues next week, unless there is a known reason the landing page fails for this specific search.
- The 4p-8p and 8p-12a blocks combined spent $92 with zero conversions this week, consistent with the pattern across the full five-week dataset. The 8p-12a block in particular has never produced a conversion across any week in the prior reports.
- "landscaping companies in my area" is the top spend keyword at $38 with zero conversions. It has appeared in the top spend list multiple times with no conversion record. This is a broad, intent-ambiguous search that could represent residential shoppers.
- "sod installers near me" had a 50 percent CTR on three clicks. That number is a data artifact of very low impression volume, not a signal.
- BH_CommercialMaint_Search produced the lowest CPA of any campaign this week at $17 on one conversion, and "commercial landscaping fort worth" was the only converting search term in the data. That campaign is losing 90 percent of auctions to rank.

### Priority Actions

1. Fix the mobile landing page or form across BH_PC_Irrigationservice and BH_LeadGen_Form_Search this week. On a phone, click a live ad from each campaign, load the page, and submit the lead form completely. Five consecutive weeks of zero mobile conversions while desktop converts at 6 to 21 percent is not a bidding pattern. If the form fails, redirects incorrectly, or the page does not render properly on mobile, fixing it is the single action most likely to recover conversions, since mobile receives the majority of clicks every week. Expected payoff: mobile conversions would likely match or exceed desktop given click volume. Confidence: high

2. Fix the landing page for "sod installation aledo tx" in BH_LeadGen_Form_Search. This keyword has scored QS 3 for at least six consecutive weeks with the landing page component rated Low every time and ad relevance rated High. The ad matches the search; the page does not. The destination page headline and body copy must explicitly reference sod installation in Aledo, TX. A QS improvement from 3 toward 6 lowers the cost per click and helps this campaign win more of the 83 percent of auctions it currently loses to rank. Expected payoff: lower CPC and improved ad position on a targeted local search. Confidence: high

3. Fix the landing page for "lawn treatment fort worth" in BH_FertWeedControl_Search by the same logic. Also six consecutive weeks at QS 3, High ad relevance, Low landing page. The destination page must lead with lawn treatment in Fort Worth. This keyword is depressing BH_FertWeedControl_Search's overall ad rank, which is losing 82 percent of auctions. Expected payoff: same as above, lower CPC and more auctions won on a relevant local keyword. Confidence: high

4. Add "lee engineering company" as a negative keyword in BH_PC_Irrigationservice this week. This is a company name search, meaning someone was looking for a specific business, not hiring an irrigation vendor. It spent $17 with zero conversions. This is clear waste with no plausible path to a conversion. Expected payoff: $17 per week redirected to searches that have actually converted. Confidence: high

5. Apply a negative 100 percent bid adjustment for the 8p-12a time block across all campaigns. Across five weeks of data this window has spent consistently with zero conversions in any campaign. Cutting it redirects that spend, roughly $29 this week, into the 8a-12p block where two of this week's three conversions occurred. With spend frozen this is a reallocation, not a reduction in capability. Expected payoff: the same budget produces more impressions during the hours that actually convert. Confidence: medium
